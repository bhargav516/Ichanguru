Projects

Inspiration Park

Initially the idea of building the park came while general discussion happened what's app group.
Few of young folks started it, kick off of the project happened on 27th June 2021, the total landscape of the area is about 9 guntas in triangle shape.
Located in East corner of the ichanguru lake.
Currently we have planted around 80+ different type of saplings which include Mahogany, Pupal tree, Neem tree, Badami, Cherry, Matti trees so on.
Inspiration park is baseline for all other upcoming projects .
Currently it is nurtured by same folks, In the upcoming years it will home for many birds and animal species
Mini forest will be the savior for upcoming generations.

Virra Huutamma Yodhas park

As we all say one brings another with the inspiration we drew from "Inspiration park" led the pathway to these project.
The design revolves around concept of fruit food park for native bird species.
While the discussions happened we agreed to dedicate the park in the name of our martyred soldiers as token of respect.
Park is located behind our government school, landscape is around 16 guntas we have utilized around 10 guntas.
The special attraction for these park is "kallukatte bavvi" which has history of over 100 years which is currently being rejuvenated by our team.
The fresh water in the Well will act as water reservoir for birds and other animals.
These Fruit park consists of several fruit bearing plants, currently we have planted 60 sapling of various fruits around 15 plus different types species.
